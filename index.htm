About Us

We are the members of various National and International Organization like IE(I), ISTE, ISOI, IETE for knowledge sharing of students and faculties. In order to conduct different performances such as Seminars, Workshops, Tech-Fests & Social Welfare activities, department has their own students association named as ENIAC for EIE branch and EONYX for ECE branch.Now,Tech-Fest of both branches is organised in the name of  GietKaryaneeti.
